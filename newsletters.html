Introduction

Welcome to the October edition of Artur's newsletter. The start of October has been very eventful for Artur. Artur recently got more hours for his job, which will allow him to save up more money for his trip in December. The trip in December is a huge event Artur is looking forward to because he gets to go out of state to see his friends in Minnesota. Minnesota is also the state Artur was born in and grew up in most of his life. Furthermore, the December trip will be amazing for Artur since he gets to experience snow again after not being able to for years.
