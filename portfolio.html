Steam portfolio

I have spent 5 years of my life modding for a Steam game called Rivals of Aether. 500k total subscribers, 180+ followers, and 40+ Steam community awards later I am still modding for this game. It all started with me wanting anime genre mods in that game and there was barely in there. I took the initiative and decided to add anime characters myself. 5 years later I am now a well known mod creator within the mod community of the game.

Character info

I have over 51 characters I have spent well over 10 hours working on for each individual character. All the modded characters are from different types of Anime.

Details

Characters like "Luffy: Bounce man" took me 8 hours to make because I have to animate each frame of the sprite using Aseprite app. Furthermore, I have to script each move (17 moves in total) and character movements (walking, running, jumping, etc). In addition, I make audio for every move, taunt, and victory screen for characters. I also use photoshop to create the cover photos for all characters.

Collections

I have 3 collections (compilations of characters all in one area) made and 2 of them are in the top 15 of all time popular in the community.

The Community

I would be lying if I said everyone in the community enjoyed my characters. However, I do have people that enjoy my content. At the end of the day I never started this hobby for any of these people, but the feedback I get currently is still nice.
